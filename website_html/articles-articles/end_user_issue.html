End user awareness issues and negotiation techniques that are applied in the software process

According to Zowghi (2015), involving end users in a software development process leads to a user satisfaction and a successful project system, as it gives software engineers a clear problem statement of user expectations, and it helps to develop appropriate strategies on how to tackle the issue. Software negotiation process can be a draining task, however one needs to read and understand the contract before rushing to purchase (Medved,2009). In addition to make a software negotiation process go smoothly one needs to consider these following steps.

Take your time before deciding. Ensure that you are well informed and understands the nature of the deal before signing on the dotted line. This will avoid misunderstandings which might cause problems in future between you and your software vendor. Take your time to familiarize yourself with the implementation process and compare what was asked with what was provided.

Negotiate everything. Be bold to negotiate essentials such as software licensing, implementation prices, maintenance fees, terms of payment and implementation duration.

Validate the requirements. Ensure that each aspect of your requirements is checked and developed. Be sure that each requirement is aligned with the system, and it is well defined and does not conflict with the others.

Plan for the future Ensure that your contract is future proofed, this means that your software vendor will have to make adjustment to the software one day should it happens that your company grows and needs a larger software or should the technology change.
